About Me

Leila Geramian, Owner and Director, received her M.A in Speech­Language Pathology from CUNY Lehman College in September, 2012. She completed her B.A in English and American Literature at New York University in January of 2008.

She is certified by the American Speech­ Language ­Hearing Association (ASHA) and is also licensed by the state of New York to practice Speech-Language Pathology.

Leila has worked in the Brooklyn elementary school system as a speech language pathologist for over 4 years. She is passionate about her role in developing expressive, receptive, and literacy skills in her clients. She aligns her work with Common Core Standards for reading, writing, listening, and speaking, and works closely with the curriculum to ensure overall academic success.

Leila is excited to bring her many years of experience to a private practice setting to provide outstanding therapy services to Williamsburg and its surrounding areas. She currently resides in Williamsburg, Brooklyn with her husband, Ramin and daughter, Darya.
